About Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun was born on April 8, 1983 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu

About Family And Education

He is the son of film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala. His paternal grandfather was the comedian Allu Ramalingaiah. His uncle is actor Chiranjeevi, and his first cousin is actor Ram Charan.
Allu Arjun completed his schooling from St. Patrick School, Chennai, and graduated from MSR College (Hyderabad) with a BBA degree.
